About the Project

CivicFix centralizes civic complaints into one platform. Citizens can report issues with photos and location, and track status until resolution.

Problem

Fragmented complaints and slow response create frustration and poor civic maintenance.

Solution

A simple reporting portal with geo-tagging, prioritization and transparent status tracking.

Key Features

Geo-tagged Reporting

Exact coordinates for each report.

Transparent Tracking

Live status visibility.

Gamified Engagement

Points & leaderboards.

Multi-channel Access

Web, app, SMS & WhatsApp-ready.

Authority Dashboard

Reports, filters & resolution panel.

Offline-friendly

Local queue for low-connectivity areas.

Why This Works

  • Simple UX with real-time location support.
  • Transparent flow from reporting to resolution.
  • Improves accountability & communication
